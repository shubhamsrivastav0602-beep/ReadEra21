Our Story

ReadEra was born from a simple idea: everyone deserves access to great books and knowledge, regardless of their financial situation. We started as a small project in 2024, driven by a passion for reading and a desire to help busy people discover valuable insights from books without spending hours reading.

Today, we've grown into a comprehensive digital library with over 10,000 book summaries across 25+ genres. Our team of dedicated readers, writers, and technologists work tirelessly to create high-quality summaries that capture the essence of each book while being easy to read and understand.

We believe that reading should be a joy, not a chore. That's why we've designed ReadEra to be beautiful, intuitive, and distraction-free. Whether you're a student, professional, or lifelong learner, ReadEra is here to help you grow.
